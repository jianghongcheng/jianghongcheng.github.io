Recent Projects

Research

My research focuses on hyperspectral image processing, image restoration, and vision transformers. Specifically, I have worked on hyperspectral pansharpening using diffusion priors and spectral transformers, thermal and NIR image super-resolution with flexible attention mechanisms, and image colorization with DCT-based networks. My work leverages Transformer architectures, diffusion models, and frequency-domain methods to improve image quality and computational efficiency in remote sensing and medical imaging applications.
